Where is Surf City?

Surf City is a small town located on the coast of North Carolina. It is situated in Pender County, between Jacksonville and Wilmington. The town is known for its beautiful white sand beaches and crystal clear waters, making it a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.

What are the main attractions in Surf City?

One of the main attractions in Surf City is, of course, the beach. With miles of pristine coastline, there are plenty of opportunities to swim, sunbathe, and surf. In addition to the beach, there are several other notable attractions in the area. These include the Surf City Pier, Soundside Park, and the Karen Beasley Sea Turtle Rescue and Rehabilitation Center.
