Introduction

If you’re a streetwear enthusiast, then you’re probably familiar with the Supreme brand. Known for their limited-edition drops and iconic collaborations, Supreme has become a staple in the fashion world. One of their most sought-after pieces is the Supreme World Map Jacket. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at this jacket and why it’s a must-have for any streetwear fan.

History of the Supreme World Map Jacket

The Supreme World Map Jacket was first released in the fall/winter 2016 collection. It was designed by Supreme’s founder James Jebbia and features a black and white world map print all over the jacket. The front of the jacket also has the Supreme logo embroidered in red. The jacket was an instant hit and sold out quickly.

Why is the Supreme World Map Jacket So Popular?

The Supreme World Map Jacket is popular for a few reasons. First, it’s a limited edition piece, which means that only a small number of jackets were made. This exclusivity drives up the demand and makes it a highly sought-after piece. Second, the jacket is made from high-quality materials, which means that it’s durable and will last for years. Finally, the jacket has a unique design that stands out from other streetwear pieces. The world map print is eye-catching and makes for a great conversation starter.

How to Style the Supreme World Map Jacket

The Supreme World Map Jacket is a statement piece, which means that it should be the focus of your outfit. You can style it in a few different ways. One option is to pair it with black jeans and a white t-shirt. This will allow the jacket to stand out and make a statement. Another option is to wear it with cargo pants and a hoodie for a more casual look.

Where to Buy the Supreme World Map Jacket

Since the Supreme World Map Jacket is a limited edition piece, it can be difficult to find. Your best bet is to check out reseller websites like Grailed or StockX. However, be prepared to pay a premium price for the jacket. It’s not uncommon for it to sell for $1,000 or more.

FAQ

The Supreme World Map Jacket is available in sizes small, medium, large, and extra-large.

What is the material of the Supreme World Map Jacket?

The Supreme World Map Jacket is made from a polyester material with a satin finish.

Is the Supreme World Map Jacket waterproof?

No, the Supreme World Map Jacket is not waterproof. It’s recommended that you avoid wearing it in rainy or wet conditions.

Conclusion

The Supreme World Map Jacket is a must-have for any streetwear fan. Its unique design, limited edition status, and high-quality materials make it a valuable addition to any collection. While it may be difficult to find and expensive to purchase, the jacket is worth the investment for its style and durability.
