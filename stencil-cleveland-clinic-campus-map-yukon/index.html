What is the Cleveland Clinic?

The Cleveland Clinic is a non-profit academic medical center located in Cleveland, Ohio. It was founded in 1921 and has since become one of the top hospitals in the world, known for its innovative research and patient care.

Where is the Cleveland Clinic located?

The Cleveland Clinic is located in the heart of Cleveland, Ohio, with multiple campuses throughout the city. The main campus, which is the focus of this article, is located at 9500 Euclid Avenue, Cleveland, OH 44195.
