Belize On World Map: A Traveler's Guide

Posted on February 28, 2023

Welcome to Belize

Located in Central America, Belize is a small country that packs a big punch. Known for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and vibrant culture, Belize is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

Where is Belize?

Belize is located on the eastern coast of Central America, bordered by Mexico to the north, Guatemala to the west and south, and the Caribbean Sea to the east.

What is the Capital of Belize?

The capital of Belize is Belmopan, a small city located in the center of the country. While it may not be as well-known as other Central American capitals, Belmopan has its own unique charm and is definitely worth a visit.

Why Visit Belize?

There are endless reasons to visit Belize, from its stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters to its ancient Mayan ruins and vibrant culture. Whether you’re looking for adventure, relaxation, or a bit of both, Belize has something for everyone.

What are the Top Tourist Attractions in Belize?

Some of the top tourist attractions in Belize include:

  • The Great Blue Hole
  • The Belize Barrier Reef
  • The ancient Mayan ruins of Tikal
  • The Cayo District rainforest
  • The Placencia Peninsula

What is the Climate Like in Belize?

The climate in Belize is tropical, with temperatures ranging from the mid-70s to the mid-80s Fahrenheit year-round. The rainy season runs from June to November, with the driest months being February to May.

What is the Currency in Belize?

The currency in Belize is the Belizean dollar (BZD), which is pegged to the US dollar at a rate of 2:1. US dollars are also widely accepted throughout the country.

What Languages are Spoken in Belize?

The official language of Belize is English, but Spanish and Kriol (a Creole language) are also widely spoken.

What Foods Should You Try in Belize?

Some of the must-try foods in Belize include:

  • Fry jacks (deep-fried dough served with beans and cheese)
  • Rice and beans (a staple dish made with red kidney beans and coconut milk)
  • Conch fritters (fried dough balls with conch meat)
  • Ceviche (raw seafood marinated in citrus juices)

How Do You Get to Belize?

There are several ways to get to Belize, including:

  • Fly into Philip S.W. Goldson International Airport in Belize City
  • Take a bus from Mexico or Guatemala
  • Take a cruise ship that stops in Belize City or Harvest Caye

Is Belize Safe for Travelers?

Overall, Belize is a safe country for travelers. However, like any destination, it’s important to take precautions and be aware of your surroundings. Petty theft and violent crime can occur, particularly in urban areas.

Conclusion

Belize may be a small country, but it’s one that’s big on adventure, culture, and natural beauty. Whether you’re looking to relax on the beach, explore ancient ruins, or sample delicious Caribbean cuisine, Belize has something to offer everyone. So why wait? Start planning your Belizean adventure today!

Q: Is Belize expensive to visit?

A: While Belize can be more expensive than some other Central American destinations, it’s still relatively affordable for travelers. Budget accommodations and street food can help keep costs down, while more upscale options are available for those looking for luxury.
