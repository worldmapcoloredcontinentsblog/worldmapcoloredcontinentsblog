Exploring Pyongyang North Korea Map In 2023

Posted on June 28, 2022

Introduction

North Korea has always been a mysterious and enigmatic country, but with the recent opening up of its borders, more and more tourists are starting to explore its hidden treasures. One of the most fascinating places to visit in North Korea is its capital city, Pyongyang. In this article, we will take a closer look at the Pyongyang North Korea map and explore some of the top attractions in this city.

What is Pyongyang?

Pyongyang is the capital city of North Korea and is known for its grand architecture, wide boulevards, and impressive monuments. The city is located on the banks of the Taedong River and is home to over 3 million people.

What can you find in Pyongyang?

Pyongyang is a city that is rich in history and culture. Some of the top attractions that you can find in this city are:

1. The Juche Tower

The Juche Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in Pyongyang. It stands at a height of 170 meters and is shaped like a torch. The tower is dedicated to the Juche ideology which was developed by the first leader of North Korea, Kim Il-sung.

2. The Arch of Triumph

The Arch of Triumph is another impressive monument that you can find in Pyongyang. It was built to commemorate the victory of the Korean people over Japan and is modeled after the Arc de Triomphe in Paris.

3. The Pyongyang Metro

The Pyongyang Metro is one of the deepest underground systems in the world, with some stations located over 100 meters below ground. The metro is known for its impressive artwork and mosaics that depict the country’s history and culture.

4. The Kumsusan Palace of the Sun

The Kumsusan Palace of the Sun is a mausoleum that is dedicated to the leaders of North Korea, Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il. The palace is located in the heart of Pyongyang and is a must-visit attraction for anyone interested in North Korean history.

5. The Mansudae Grand Monument

The Mansudae Grand Monument is a giant statue of the country’s leaders, Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il. The monument is located in Mansudae Hill and is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Pyongyang.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are many interesting things to see and do in Pyongyang. Whether you are interested in history, culture, or architecture, this city has something for everyone. So, if you are planning a trip to North Korea, make sure to add Pyongyang to your itinerary and explore all that this fascinating city has to offer.

Question & Answer:

Q: Is it safe to travel to North Korea?

A: While North Korea is generally safe for tourists, it is important to be aware of the rules and regulations that are in place. Tourists are not allowed to travel outside of designated areas without a guide, and any behavior that is deemed disrespectful to the country’s leaders or culture can result in severe consequences.

Q: Can I use my cell phone in North Korea?

A: Foreigners are not allowed to use their cell phones in North Korea. However, some hotels in Pyongyang have internet access, and you can use the hotel’s computer to check your email or make phone calls.

Q: What is the best time of year to visit Pyongyang?

A: The best time to visit Pyongyang is during the spring or fall when the weather is mild and the city’s parks and gardens are in full bloom. Avoid visiting during the summer months as the weather can be hot and humid, and during the winter months, the city can be covered in snow and ice.
