Discover The Hidden Gems Of Fort Scott, Kansas With Our Map

Posted on June 29, 2022

Introduction

If you’re planning a trip to Fort Scott, Kansas, then you won’t want to miss out on some of the hidden gems that this charming town has to offer. From historic landmarks to outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone here. To help you make the most of your visit, we’ve created a detailed map that highlights some of the top attractions in the area.

What to See and Do in Fort Scott

The first stop on your tour of Fort Scott should be the Fort Scott National Historic Site. This well-preserved fort played an important role in the Civil War and offers a fascinating glimpse into the past. Next, head to the Gordon Parks Museum to learn about the life and work of this celebrated photographer, filmmaker, and writer. If you’re looking for outdoor adventure, then head to Gunn Park. This beautiful park offers miles of hiking and biking trails, as well as a fishing pond and picnic areas. The Bourbon County State Lake is another great spot for fishing and camping.

Where to Eat and Shop

When it comes to dining in Fort Scott, you won’t be disappointed. The Boiler Room Brewhaus is a must-visit for craft beer enthusiasts, while the Crooner’s Lounge is the perfect spot for a romantic dinner. For some delicious BBQ, head to Sharky’s Pub & Grub. If you’re in the mood for some retail therapy, then head to the historic downtown area. Here, you’ll find a variety of shops selling everything from antiques to artisanal crafts. Don’t miss out on the chance to pick up some handmade soap from the Lavender Patch Farm.

Where to Stay

If you’re planning on spending the night in Fort Scott, then you’ll find plenty of options to suit all budgets. The Courtland Hotel & Spa is a luxurious option, while the Lyons Twin Mansions offers a more intimate experience. The Fort Scott Inn is a great choice for budget-conscious travelers.

Conclusion

Fort Scott may be a small town, but it’s definitely worth a visit. Whether you’re interested in history, outdoor adventure, or just good food and shopping, there’s something for everyone here. Be sure to check out our map to help you plan your visit and make the most of your time in this charming Kansas town.
