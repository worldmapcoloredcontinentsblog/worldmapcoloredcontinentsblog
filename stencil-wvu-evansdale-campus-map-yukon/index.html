WVU Evansdale Campus Map: Navigating Your Way Around If you are a student or faculty member at West Virginia University, you know how important it is to have a good understanding of the Evansdale Campus. With so many buildings, parking lots, and amenities to navigate, it can be overwhelming at times. That’s why we’ve put together this guide to help you make the most of your time on the Evansdale Campus. First, let’s start with the basics. The Evansdale Campus is located on the eastern side of the WVU campus in Morgantown, West Virginia. It is home to the College of Engineering and Mineral Resources, the College of Business and Economics, and the WVU Health Sciences Center. The campus also features a variety of dining options, including a food court, coffee shops, and fast food restaurants. Now that you know the general layout of the campus, let’s talk about the best ways to get around. One of the most important tools you can use is the WVU Evansdale Campus Map. This map shows all of the buildings, parking lots, and other important locations on the campus. You can access the map online, or pick up a paper copy at the WVU Welcome Center. When using the map, be sure to pay attention to the building numbers. Each building is labeled with a number that corresponds to the numbers on the map. This will help you find your way around more easily. You can also use the map to locate the nearest parking lot to your destination. Another important feature of the Evansdale Campus is the PRT, or Personal Rapid Transit. The PRT is a system of automated trains that run on elevated tracks throughout the campus. It is free to use for WVU students and faculty, and provides a quick and easy way to get from one side of the campus to the other. If you prefer to walk, there are plenty of sidewalks and pathways throughout the campus. Be sure to wear comfortable shoes, as some of the pathways are hilly and can be challenging to navigate. If you need assistance getting around, you can contact the WVU Transportation and Parking office for help. Now let’s take a closer look at some of the key locations on the Evansdale Campus. The Engineering Sciences Building is home to the College of Engineering and Mineral Resources. It features state-of-the-art laboratories and classrooms, as well as a variety of offices and meeting spaces. The Business and Economics Building houses the College of Business and Economics. It features a variety of classrooms, computer labs, and meeting spaces, as well as the WVU Business Learning Center. The Health Sciences Center is home to the WVU School of Medicine, the School of Dentistry, and the School of Nursing. It also features a variety of research labs and clinical facilities. If you’re looking for a bite to eat, there are plenty of options on the Evansdale Campus. The Mountainlair Food Court features a variety of fast food restaurants, while the Evansdale Crossing food court offers healthier options like salads and sandwiches. You can also find coffee shops and convenience stores throughout the campus. Finally, let’s address some common questions about the Evansdale Campus: Q: How do I get a parking pass for the campus? A: You can purchase a parking pass online through the WVU Transportation and Parking website. Q: Is there a shuttle service on the campus? A: Yes, the PRT serves as a shuttle service and is free to use for WVU students and faculty. Q: Are there any quiet study areas on the campus? A: Yes, the Evansdale Library offers a variety of quiet study spaces and meeting rooms. In conclusion, the Evansdale Campus is a key part of the WVU community. By using the campus map and familiarizing yourself with the key locations, you can make the most of your time on the campus.
