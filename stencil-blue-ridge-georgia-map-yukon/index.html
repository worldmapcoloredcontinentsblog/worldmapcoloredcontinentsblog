Blue Ridge is a small town located in Fannin County, Georgia. It is known for its stunning scenery, including the Blue Ridge Mountains, the Toccoa River, and Lake Blue Ridge. If you’re visiting Blue Ridge, Georgia, there are several must-see attractions that you won’t want to miss. And for a taste of local history, you can visit the Fannin County Heritage Museum or the Blue Ridge Mountains Arts Association. Where can you find the best food in Blue Ridge, Georgia?
