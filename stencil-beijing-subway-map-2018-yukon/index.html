Beijing Subway Map 2018: Navigating the World’s Largest Subway System If you’re planning a trip to Beijing, one of the first things you’ll need to familiarize yourself with is the city’s subway system. With 22 lines and over 370 stations, the Beijing Subway is the largest subway system in the world. But don’t worry, with a little bit of preparation and a good Beijing subway map 2018, you’ll be able to navigate the city like a pro. Here are some tips and information to help you make the most of your Beijing Subway experience: 1. Download a Beijing Subway Map 2018 App Before you even arrive in Beijing, you can download a Beijing Subway map app to your smartphone or tablet. This will allow you to familiarize yourself with the system and plan your routes ahead of time. Some popular apps include Beijing Subway Map, Metro China Subway, and Explore Beijing Subway. 2. Know the Lines and Stations The Beijing Subway is divided into 22 lines, each with its own unique color and number. It’s important to familiarize yourself with the lines and the stations you’ll be using, so you don’t get lost or confused. Some of the most popular lines for tourists include Line 1, which runs east to west through the city center, and Line 2, which runs north to south through the city. 3. Buy a Beijing Subway Card If you’re planning to use the Beijing Subway frequently, it’s a good idea to buy a Beijing Subway Card. This will save you time and money, as you won’t have to buy individual tickets for each ride. You can purchase a card at any subway station or convenience store, and then top it up with money as needed. 4. Know the Peak Hours The Beijing Subway can get very crowded during peak hours, so it’s a good idea to avoid these times if possible. Peak hours are typically from 7-9 am and 5-7 pm on weekdays. If you do need to travel during these times, be prepared for crowded trains and long waits. 5. Be Prepared for Security Checks In recent years, the Beijing Subway has implemented security checks at all stations. This means that you’ll need to go through a metal detector and have your bags scanned before entering the station. Be prepared for this process and allow extra time for your travels. 6. Use the English Signs While many of the signs in the Beijing Subway are in Chinese, there are also English signs available. Look for signs that say “Exit” (for leaving the station) or “Entrance” (for entering the station). There are also signs that show the line and station names in English. 7. Be Mindful of Your Surroundings As with any public transportation system, it’s important to be mindful of your surroundings when using the Beijing Subway. Keep an eye on your belongings and be aware of any suspicious activity around you. 8. Use the Beijing Subway for Tourist Attractions The Beijing Subway is a great way to get to many of the city’s top tourist attractions, such as the Forbidden City, Tiananmen Square, and the Summer Palace. Use your Beijing Subway map 2018 app to plan your route and save money on transportation. 9. Take Advantage of the Free Wi-Fi Many of the stations on the Beijing Subway offer free Wi-Fi, which can be a great way to stay connected while on the go. Look for signs that say “Subway Wi-Fi” and connect to the network to start browsing. 10. Ask for Help if You Need It Finally, don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Most of the staff at the Beijing Subway speak at least some English and are happy to assist you with your travels. Overall, the Beijing Subway is a convenient and affordable way to get around the city. With a little bit of preparation and a good Beijing Subway map 2018, you’ll be able to navigate the world’s largest subway system like a pro.
