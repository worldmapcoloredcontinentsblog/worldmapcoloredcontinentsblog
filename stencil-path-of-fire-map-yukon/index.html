Introduction

The Path of Fire is an exciting expansion pack for Guild Wars 2 that was released in 2017. The expansion pack introduced a new map with a variety of new locations, enemies, and challenges. In this article, we will take a closer look at the Path of Fire map and provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you navigate through it with ease.

Getting Started

To access the Path of Fire map, you must first complete the “Sparking the Flame” story mission. After completing this mission, you will be able to access the new map from the Crystal Oasis waypoint in the Crystal Desert.

Map Overview

The Path of Fire map is divided into several regions, including the Crystal Oasis, Desert Highlands, Elon Riverlands, The Desolation, and Domain of Vabbi. Each region has its unique challenges, enemies, and rewards. The map also includes several points of interest, waypoints, and vistas, which allow you to explore the map more easily.

Points of Interest

Points of Interest are marked with a star icon on the map. These are locations that are worth visiting, as they often contain valuable loot, enemies to defeat, or interesting stories to uncover. Some points of interest may also be hidden, and you will need to complete a series of tasks or puzzles to access them.

Waypoints

Waypoints are marked with a blue icon on the map. These allow you to travel quickly between different locations on the map. You can use waypoints to travel to previously visited locations, or to travel to new areas that you have not yet explored.

Vistas

Vistas are marked with a camera icon on the map. These are scenic locations that provide stunning views of the surrounding landscape. To access a vista, you will need to climb to a high location or complete a jumping puzzle.

Enemies

The Path of Fire map is filled with a variety of enemies, including giant sand wurms, branded creatures, and forged soldiers. These enemies are often challenging to defeat and require careful planning and strategy. However, defeating these enemies can reward you with valuable loot and experience points.

Challenges

The Path of Fire map also includes several challenges, including jumping puzzles, races, and bounties. Jumping puzzles require you to navigate through a series of obstacles to reach a specific location, while races require you to complete a course as quickly as possible. Bounties involve defeating a specific enemy or group of enemies, and they often reward you with valuable loot and experience points.
